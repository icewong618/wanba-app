# v5.246 Merchant Feature Center Refinement

- Merchant pages no longer show the redundant certification message.
- The top of Feature Center now lists the four default merchant capabilities: merchant page foundation, AI publishing, merchant store, and membership.
- All optional modules are grouped as Advanced Features.
- Future modules remain gray and show a gradual-rollout message when selected.
- Customer-facing advanced modules will use a prefilled-but-editable Scoop City nickname and require a phone number before submission. Future payment success is the final confirmation of a real order or registration. Names and phone numbers are visible only to the relevant merchant.
